# Memory

You shouldn't have to remember that the project deadline moved to April 3rd, or that your client prefers to be called "Dr. Patel," or that the budget was approved last Tuesday. FlowMail's memory system tracks these facts for you — and uses them to make every AI action smarter.

## Automatic Knowledge Extraction

As emails flow through FlowMail, the AI identifies important facts: names, dates, decisions, preferences, project details, and commitments. These are extracted as memory candidates — potential additions to your personal knowledge base.

## You Stay in Control

Memory candidates are always presented for your review before being saved. FlowMail never quietly adds information to your knowledge base. You see what was extracted, you approve or reject it, and you can edit the wording before it's stored.

## Memory Health

Each memory has a health score based on three factors:

- **Confidence** — How certain the AI is that this fact is correct
- **Recency** — How recently the information was confirmed or referenced
- **Access frequency** — How often this memory has been used in AI actions

Stale or low-confidence memories naturally fade in influence, while frequently validated facts remain strong.

## Time-Limited Facts

Some information has an expiration date. "The proposal deadline is March 15th" is only useful until March 15th. FlowMail tracks these time-limited facts and automatically reduces their relevance after they expire — keeping your knowledge base current without manual cleanup.

## Superseding Memories

When information changes, FlowMail handles the update gracefully. If an old memory says "the budget is $50,000" and a new email says "the budget has been increased to $75,000," the new memory supersedes the old one. You always have the latest information.

## Search and Browse

Find any memory by searching with keywords, or browse your knowledge base by entity — see everything FlowMail knows about a specific person, project, or topic.

## Automatic Consolidation

Over time, you may accumulate overlapping or duplicate memories. FlowMail periodically reviews your knowledge base and merges related facts, keeping your memory clean and efficient.

## Powering Smarter AI

Your memory isn't just a reference for you — it's a reference for FlowMail's AI. When drafting responses, classifying emails, or extracting tasks, the AI draws on your memories to make better, more informed decisions. The more FlowMail remembers, the more personalized and accurate it becomes.
