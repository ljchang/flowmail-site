# Email Triage

Every email that arrives in your inbox needs a decision: act on it, read it later, or ignore it. FlowMail's AI triage makes that decision for you — instantly and accurately — so you can focus on what actually matters.

## Automatic Classification

When new emails arrive, FlowMail's AI reads each one and classifies it by the action you need to take:

- **No Action** — Informational emails that don't require a response
- **Simple Reply** — A quick response is all that's needed
- **Multi-Step Response** — Requires thought, research, or a longer reply
- **Review Needed** — Documents, proposals, or content to review
- **Scheduling** — Meeting requests or calendar coordination
- **Delegation** — Something better handled by someone else

When classification determines that action is needed, FlowMail creates a plan to handle it — connecting directly to the [Plans](/docs/features/plans) system so nothing falls through the cracks.

You never have to manually sort your inbox again.

## Important vs. Other

FlowMail sorts every email into two buckets: **Important** and **Other**. Important emails are the ones that need your attention or action — a question from a colleague, a decision that's waiting on you, a deadline approaching. Everything else — newsletters, notifications, FYI messages, automated alerts — lands in Other.

What counts as "important" is personal, and FlowMail learns your definition over time. Every time you correct a classification, the AI adjusts its reasoning. After a few days of use, it knows that emails from your CEO are always important and that weekly digest emails never are.

## Smart Card Assignment

Every email is automatically routed to the right [Card](/docs/features/cards) based on its content. An email about Project Alpha goes to your Project Alpha card. A message from your manager goes to the right relationship card. You always see emails in the context where they belong.

## The AI Learns From You

Disagree with a classification? Just correct it. FlowMail doesn't just record your correction — it analyzes why it was wrong and adjusts its reasoning. Over time, triage becomes increasingly accurate and personalized to your workflow. Learn more about how this works on the [Learning & Self-Improvement](/docs/features/learning) page.

## Fast Full-Text Search

Need to find an old email? FlowMail includes a fast local search engine powered by Tantivy. Search across all your emails instantly — no waiting for a server round-trip.

## Thread-Based Conversations

Emails are displayed as threaded conversations, so you always see the full context of a discussion. No more scrolling through disconnected messages trying to piece together what was said.

## Keyboard-First Processing

Power through your inbox with keyboard shortcuts:

- **j / k** to navigate between emails
- **e** to archive the selected email
- **r** to reply
- **c** to open the card picker
- **Shift + Arrow** to multi-select emails for batch actions

The goal is to get through triage as fast as you think — no mouse required. See the full list of shortcuts on the [Keyboard Shortcuts](/docs/features/keyboard-shortcuts) page.

## Batch Smart Commands

When you select multiple emails, a batch bar appears where you can type natural language commands. Say "important" to reclassify them all, "delegate" to flag them for delegation, "assign to Project Alpha" to route them to a card, or "schedule" to mark them for calendar follow-up. FlowMail interprets the command and applies the right action to every selected email at once.
