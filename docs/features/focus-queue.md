# Focus Queue

Your inbox is full of hidden to-dos. Someone asks you to review a document. A client needs a proposal by Friday. A teammate is waiting on your approval. The Focus Queue surfaces these buried action items so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Automatic Task Extraction

FlowMail's AI reads your emails and extracts the things you actually need to do. Instead of scanning every message yourself, you get a clean list of next actions — each one linked back to the email it came from.

## Task Types

Inspired by [Getting Things Done (GTD)](https://gettingthingsdone.com/), every task in the Focus Queue has a clear type that tells you what kind of action is needed:

- **Compose** — Write a reply. This is the most common email action and the one most likely to pile up.
- **Action** — Do something specific that isn't email-related (review a document, update a spreadsheet, make a phone call).
- **Research** — You can't act yet because you need more information. Investigate or clarify first.
- **Waiting** — You've delegated this or you're blocked on someone else. Check back later.
- **Schedule** — Create a calendar event or coordinate a meeting time.
- **Delegate** — Forward to someone better suited to handle it. The task moves to your Waiting queue so you remember to follow up.

## The Clarify Step

Each task has a text input where you decide what needs to happen. This is the GTD "clarify" step — the moment where a vague obligation becomes a concrete next action. Instead of staring at "Re: Q3 Planning" and wondering what to do, you write "Send revised timeline to Jordan by Thursday."

## Priority Bands

Tasks are organized into priority bands so you always know what to work on next:

- **Urgent** — Needs your attention right now
- **High** — Handle today
- **Normal** — This week

## Pin to Your Agenda

Have a task that keeps getting buried? Pin it to your agenda so it stays visible at the top of your Focus Queue until you're done with it.

## Feedback That Teaches

When you dismiss a task, FlowMail asks why:

- **Not actionable** — This wasn't really a to-do
- **Wrong card** — The task was assigned to the wrong context

This feedback goes directly into FlowMail's [learning system](/docs/features/learning), so the AI gets better at extracting the right tasks over time.

## Quick-Add

Need to add a task manually? The quick-add widget lets you create tasks on the fly without leaving the Focus Queue.
