## Installation

1. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
2. Drag **FlowMail** into your **Applications** folder.
3. Open FlowMail from Applications. On first launch, macOS may show a security warning — click **Open** to proceed. The app is code-signed with an Apple Developer certificate and notarized by Apple.

Once installed, follow the [Connecting Accounts](./connecting-accounts.md) guide to set up your email and AI.

## System Requirements

- **macOS 13 (Ventura)** or later
- **Apple Silicon** (M1, M2, M3, M4) — Intel Macs are not supported
- At least 4 GB of available disk space (more if using local AI models)

## Updates

FlowMail checks for updates automatically every few hours. When an update is available, you'll see a notification in the app. Updates are verified with Ed25519 cryptographic signatures before installation.

You can also check manually via the **Check for Updates** menu item.
